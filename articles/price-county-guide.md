# Price County Wisconsin: Your Outdoor Adventure Guide

Over 50 lakes, 350+ miles of snowmobile trails, and the Chequamegon-Nicolet National Forest.

## Fishing
Walleye, northern pike, bass, and trout across dozens of lakes.

## Snowmobiling
Groomed trails open mid-December through mid-March.

## Camping
Primitive forest sites to full-service campgrounds.

Visit [pricecounty.fun](https://pricecounty.fun). After your adventure, stop by [Borrachos Bar and Grill](https://borrachos.bar).
